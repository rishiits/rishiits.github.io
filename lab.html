Mature Research

We have a few avenues of research that are mature or functionally-mature. These include:

Modular Nanorobotics

We've identified a completely novel method to do modular nanorobotics that utilizes discrete units of C60 Buckminstefullerene. Our research encompasses accurate binary-to-analog communication and coordinated, mass-scale movement.

Intelligent Evolution

Our computational division explores self-iterating intelligent systems- systems that increase or modify their intelligence and internal substructure. This is a cornerstone of our research, especially as we move to increasingly modular and binary-communicable nanorobotics systems that have autonomous abilities of their own.
