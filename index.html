Why Nanotechnology?

We believe creating fundamentally powerful, precisely controlled technology is, in most cases, better than the alternative. We believe subnanotechnology and its offshoots are, in conjunction with modern advances in AI, twin revolutions that will profoundly improve civilization if done correctly. Hume was formed, first and foremost, to do social good.
