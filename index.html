Why Nanotechnology?

Technology at the nanoscale and below are thing's we're willing to bet will be amongst the greatest revolutions in the century, and we're working rapidly and intelligently to shorten timelines by a significant factor. Classical nanoscale storage systems (DNA), molecular manufacturers (ribosomes) and modular nanorobots (cells) are the foundation of all intelligent life that, to our knowledge, has ever existed. We're actively working on methods far more powerful and compact than these. Due to the increasingly apparent onslaught of intelligence more capable than human intellect, a suitably versatile substrate is a must to fully realize its capabilities.
