Simulation Software for Atoms and Molecules

Nanoengineer-1

Nanoengineer-1 is a CAD software that's made to model atom-scale interactions of certain classes of nanomaterials and nanotechnology. It has a great interface, and was open sourced when the parent company went under. Our company assists in the maintenance of this project.

Nanoengineer-1

AtomCAD

Our company, partnered with independent researchers, are making a GPU-efficient CAD software for simulating millions of atoms. A WIP
